🧠 Reflection: Mock Interview

Introduction

This reflection examines my performance in a formal mock interview conducted as part of the Project Presentation assessment. The session served as a platform to demonstrate my professional readiness, technical alignment with the mobile application development industry, and ability to communicate complex concepts under pressure. By applying the STAR method, I critically evaluate my delivery, body language, and response structure to identify areas for growth.

STAR Reflection

Situation: Simulated remote job interview for an IT/App Development role.

Task: Present my passion, technical background, and readiness as a graduate developer.

Action: Maintained professional appearance, used structured responses, and emphasized key points through gestures and logical breakdowns.

Result: Communicated strong technical awareness but presentation was shorter than required (2:46).

Critical Evaluation

Strengths: Professional tone, structured delivery, forward-thinking insight (AI awareness).

Weaknesses: Nervousness, lack of detailed real-world examples, shorter duration.

Areas for Improvement

Improve confidence, expand STAR responses with real examples, and prepare additional content to meet time requirements.

Conclusion

This mock interview was a valuable benchmark and highlighted key areas for improvement toward professional readiness.

🌐 Reflection: GitHub Pages

Introduction
