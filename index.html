🧠 Reflection: Mock Interview

Introduction

This reflection examines my performance in a formal mock interview conducted as part of the Project Presentation assessment. The session served as a platform to demonstrate my professional readiness, technical alignment with the mobile application development industry, and ability to communicate complex concepts under pressure.

STAR Reflection

Situation: Simulated remote job interview for an IT/App Development role.

Task: Present my passion, technical background, and readiness as a graduate developer.

Action: Maintained professional appearance, used structured responses, and emphasized key points clearly.

Result: Communicated strong technical awareness but presentation was slightly shorter than required (2:46).

Critical Evaluation

Strengths: Professional tone, structured delivery, strong technical awareness.

Weaknesses: Nervousness, limited real-world examples, shorter duration.

Areas for Improvement

Improve confidence, expand STAR responses with real examples, and practice timing for completeness.

Conclusion

This mock interview provided valuable insight into my readiness for professional environments and highlighted areas for improvement.

Introduction
